Brooklyn Buschenschank is a farmhouse style tavern inspired by the Alto-Adige region in Italy. The main dining room and bar boast plenty of exposed brick and reclaimed wood providing a working fireplace, communal tables and an open kitchen.

It is ideal for a gathering of 50 to 100 people. In the warmer months the large opened windows give an open patio feel.

Downstairs, the Ratskeller is ideal for an intimate gathering of 25 to 50 people. The Ratskeller includes a working fireplace featuring exquisite exposed brick detailing with striking archways in the classical European style.
