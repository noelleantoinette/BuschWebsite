Buschenschank features a flexible menu of Northern Italian and German cuisine, making it ideal to suit a large range of party sizes and needs. From birthday parties to wedding receptions and office gatherings. Buschenschank caters to every occasion.

Each menu can be tailored to suit vegetarian guests. We proudly serve South Brooklyn Pizza. Buschenschank offers group dining service for dinners and lunches. We create platters of your selected menu items or a buffet service, perfect for cocktail receptions and childrens parties.

Every event is catered by members of staff, to accommodate your every need. Custom cocktail menus and extra staff are available for an additional fee.
